    We use Google Analytics, a web analysis service from Google Inc. (‘Google’). Google uses cookies. Google uses this information on our behalf to assess how users make use of our online service, to produce reports about activities within this online service, and to generate further services connected with the use of this online service and internet usage. From the processed data we are able to create pseudonymous user profiles of users. We only use Google Analytics with active IP anonymisation. This means that the IP addresses of users are shortened by Google within EU member states and in other states that have signed the European economic zone treaty. The IP address received from the user’s browser is not combined with any other data from Google. The user can prevent cookies from being saved with the corresponding browser software settings.
